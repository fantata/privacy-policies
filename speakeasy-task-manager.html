Privacy Policy for SpeakEasy

Last Updated: January 4, 2025

Simple Summary: SpeakEasy stores all your data locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We don't collect, access, or transmit any of your personal information. Your tasks and voice recordings never leave your devices.

1. Information We Don't Collect

SpeakEasy is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Track your usage or behavior
  • Store any data on our servers
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Share any information with third parties
  • Display advertisements
  • Require you to create an account

2. Local Data Storage

SpeakEasy stores your tasks locally on your Mac in a plain text file using the todo.txt format. This data is stored:

  • In your iCloud Drive (if iCloud is enabled)
  • In your local Documents folder (if iCloud is not enabled)
  • In a human-readable, portable text format

You have complete control over this file and can edit, move, or delete it at any time.

3. Voice Recognition

SpeakEasy uses Apple's built-in Speech Recognition framework to convert your voice into text. This processing:

  • Occurs entirely on your device (on-device processing)
  • Does not send audio to external servers
  • Is subject to Apple's privacy policies for Speech Recognition
  • Requires your explicit permission via macOS system permissions

Voice recordings are processed in real-time and are not stored by SpeakEasy.

4. iCloud Sync

If you choose to enable iCloud sync, your tasks are stored in your personal iCloud account:

  • Data is synced between your devices using your iCloud account
  • All syncing is handled directly by Apple's iCloud infrastructure
  • We have no access to your iCloud data
  • Your data is subject to Apple's iCloud privacy policy

5. Permissions

SpeakEasy requests the following macOS permissions:

  • Microphone Access: Required to record your voice for task creation
  • Speech Recognition: Required to convert your voice to text
  • iCloud Access: Optional, for syncing tasks across your devices

You can revoke these permissions at any time through macOS System Settings.

6. Data Portability

Your data is stored in the open todo.txt format, which means:

  • You can easily export your tasks by copying the todo.txt file
  • Your data is compatible with other todo.txt applications
  • You can read and edit your tasks with any text editor
  • No proprietary formats or lock-in

7. Children's Privacy

SpeakEasy does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is designed to be used safely by users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of SpeakEasy after any changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Open Source

SpeakEasy's source code may be made available for review, allowing you to verify our privacy claims and see exactly how your data is handled.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or SpeakEasy's privacy practices, please contact:

Email: [Your contact email]

11. Your Rights

Because all your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access your data: Find your todo.txt file in iCloud Drive or Documents
  • Delete your data: Delete the todo.txt file or uninstall the app
  • Export your data: Copy the todo.txt file to any location
  • Modify your data: Edit the todo.txt file with any text editor
Bottom Line: SpeakEasy is a privacy-first application. Your tasks stay on your devices, we don't collect any data, and you maintain full control over your information at all times.
